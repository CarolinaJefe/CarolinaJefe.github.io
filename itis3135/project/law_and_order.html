LAW & ORDER

Basis

This webpage outlines some of the rules we enforce at Space Invaders. If you're thinking about joining us, take note of the principles we uphold. Please note that these rules are not exhaustive. For additional information, we encourage you to join our Discord community, accessible via the Contact webpage.

General

  • Discrimination, bullying, or disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Use the in-game chat or Discord to communicate effectively with clan members.
  • Members are expected to maintain weekly activity, with exceptions granted for participation in wars.
  • Promotions are granted based on merit; please refrain from requesting them.
  • Rushed bases are not tolerated.

War

  • Opt in for war if you're available; opt out if you're not. It's as simple as that.
  • Always use your first attack against your mirror base.
  • The top 3 bases should donate troops to the rest of the clan members.
  • Do not attack bases that have been flagged.
  • Check for Clan Castle troops before launching your attack!

Clan Capital

  • Prioritize spending your Capital Gold on essential buildings first.
  • Do not follow up with an attack after someone has finished theirs unless they have used all of their attacks.
  • Make sure to use all of your attacks.

Clan Games

  • Complete Builder Hall challenges first as they can be completed more quickly.
  • Ensure a minimum of 2000 points or higher.
  • Participate!
