Website Evaluations

MEDIUM

Medium's website serves as a platform for human stories and ideas, encouraging individuals to share insightful perspectives, valuable knowledge, and life wisdom with the world. This is an example of a GOOD website.

  • C: There is a fine distinction in the semantics of the HTML page. Simplicity is in effect, with colors signaling different sections of the page.
  • R: Articles are organized within columns and remain consistent across other webpages. Font style is identical, and sizing is appropriate.
  • A: The usage of columns maintains page alignment, and window resizing has no effect on the webpage display.
  • P: The content is evenly spaced with sufficient margins, making it difficult to misinterpret the distinction between sections.
